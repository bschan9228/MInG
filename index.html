Master Input Gadget

A portable password management device

Byron Chan, Daniel Pak, Duanhui Li

ECE 196 Winter 2023

Problem Definition

Between Gradescope, Canvas, TritonLink, etc., UCSD students have too many unique passwords to remember, which are necessary when using ITS computers. Itâ€™s a hassle to go through your phone or sign into an extension to access credentials.

Proposed Idea

A USB hardware password manager. It utilizes the ESP32-S3 SoC containing built-in full-speed USB OTG, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth. It is also capable of plugging into both USB-A and USB-C host devices.
